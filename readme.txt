== Description ==

Bizify.me is a complete e-commerce solution with an included payment gateway that supports VISA, MasterCard and mobile payments by SMS.

You can for example sell your photos, videos, music, e-books, documents or software and you don’t have to run your own business or be a professional to sell your digital products – at Bizify.me everyone is welcome!

At Bizify.me we believe in simplicity. Therefore, you pay no setup fees, no monthly fees, no fixed transaction fees and get access to all features without any additional costs – **and you always keep between 70 % to 95 % of the revenue.**

To get a quote regarding the revenue percentage, please contact us via our *[contact form](https://www.bizify.me/contact/)* and tell us what you plan to sell.

The handle your account from your mobile device, please install our *[iPhone/iPad app](https://itunes.apple.com/app/bizify.me/id635602512)* or our *[Android app](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=me.bizify.application&hl=en)*.

== Installation ==

1. Upload all files to the '/wp-content/plugins/' directory.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.

At the moment the Bizify.me plugin only supports the classic editor so please make sure that you have added support for the classic editor in your WordPress installation..

If you use the Bizify.me plugin together with the Infinite Scroll plugin, please add *BizifyConvertLinks();* to the callback section of Infinite Scroll.

If you use the Bizify.me plugin together with the Lazy Load plugin, please remember to add *no-lazy* at *Skip images with classes* to the settings of Lazy Load.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Do I need to do anything else except to install the plugin and create an account on Bizify.me? =

You do not have to do anything else for the store to function properly. That's the idea behind Bizify.me that it should be easy to sell a digital product.

Bizify.me is a complete store where the payment gateway is included. Customers can pay with their Visa or MasterCard and customers in some countries can also pay with their mobile phone by sending a SMS message.

= Do I need to have a PayPal account or any other kind of account at a payment gateway to handle the transaction? =

No, you do not need a PayPal account or any other account at a payment gateway. Bizify.me is a payment gateway and we handle the transaction for you.

Bizify.me also handle all payments to your bank account and we can do a bank transfer to almost all countries worldwide.

= Do you have any other FAQ page besides the one here at WordPress.org? =

Yes, we do. You can find our regular FAQ page at: <https://www.bizify.me/help/>

You can also read our *[Getting started](https://www.bizify.me/getting-started)* page and also look at our *[Examples](https://www.bizify.me/getting-started/examples/)*.

= I need to ask you some more question about your service. Can I get in contact with you? =

Yes, you can. You can get in contact with us at: <https://www.bizify.me/contact/>

= Can I also become an affiliate to Bizify.me? =

Yes, you can! We have an extensive affiliate system and we provide affiliate accounts to individual users and also, for example, to operators of large blog network. Please [contact us](https://www.bizify.me/contact/) and we'll find a deal that we both feel satisfied with!

= Can I use Bizify.me together with Infinite Scroll? =

Yes, you can! But if you do, please remember to add *BizifyConvertLinks();* to the callback section of Infinite Scroll.

= Can I use Bizify.me together with Lazy Load? =

Maybe, but we don't recommend it! If you try, please remember to add *no-lazy* at *Skip images with classes* to the settings of Lazy Load.
